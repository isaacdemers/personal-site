Context

The Situation

Latham Farms is a grand opening assignment — a different kind of challenge than what came before. At Mall of Louisiana and Altamesa, the work was stabilization: walking into restaurants that had declined and rebuilding them. Here, the work is foundation-setting. There is no culture to fix. There is culture to build from the first day.

The GOS role carries the same responsibility for people and results, but the context is new: a brand new restaurant, a team that has never worked together, and a community that is experiencing Chick-fil-A for the first time.
